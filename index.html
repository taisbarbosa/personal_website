Short Biographi

I'm Taís Barbosa Rodrigues, brazilian, born in Mato Verde, a city situated to the north of the state of Mines Gerais, in 23 of july of 2004. In 2006, together with my siblings and parents, I moved to Atibaia in state of the São Paulo, because of my parents' works.
Mato Verde, beloved city, I having visited many times, and where live many loved ones.
Atibaia and the surrounding area is where I spent from childhood to adulthood. School, friends, formations, church, life challenges... and most importantly, HOME!
Currently, I'm living away from my family in Campo Mourão, Paraná, for educational purposes.
